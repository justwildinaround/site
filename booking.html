Booking Request
Pick a date + duration first — then choose a start time from what’s available.
Submitting creates a 45-minute soft-hold. If we don’t approve in time, it automatically frees up.
How approval works
You submit → we get an email → we click Approve/Reject → you get an email back.
Pending hold: the requested slot is held for 45 minutes.
Approve: locks the time on the site + sends you a confirmation email.
Reject: frees the slot instantly + sends you a rejection email.
